About Me

I used to make suits, and now I write code.
I originally majored in Electrical Engineering at Tufts University, before I left to pursue my interests in the fashion industry. After studying Menswear at the Fashion Institute of Technology, I apprenticed at a bespoke atelier in Brooklyn and became a traditionally trained tailor.
During the pandemic, while the atelier was closed, I decided to apply my engineering background to develop a pattern-making app in Python. And, through that experience, I discovered my love of coding.
